About Mara Hills Hotel

Mara Hills hotel is a tourist camp located in the heart of Masai Mara National Reserve. It is located in Sekenani gate which makes it the first option for all tourists that visits Masai Mara game reserve. We provide top notch services only available in five star hotels in developed countries. We provide different cuisines across the world to carter for our clients who come from different parts of the world. We have an outstanding staff ranging from chefs to experienced tour guides who are ready and willing to make your stay at Mara Hills Hotel a memorable moment.
